URGENT MESSAGE ABOUT THE FUTURE OF THE POPSICL CHANNEL

by khampa

I (khampa) am no longer going to appear in PopsicL's videos.

No, it's not because we got into a fight.

No, it's not because of personal reasons.

It's because I no longer find gaming interesting. And no, this isn't one of those rants where somebody craps on the current game industry, even though I could write crap about that too.

I don't like gaming anymore, because I don't find any form of games to be fun at all, not even board games.

Also whenever I think I started to like gaming again after I've found a very interesting and fun game, I just get bored of it after a few days again.

I don't even know why this has happened only over the past year or so. I just feel like whenever I'm playing a game, I'm forcing myself to do so.

But why am I only quitting now, when this has been happening for over a year?

Because while playing with friends it was still kind of easy to hide that I would rather be doing anything else but gaming at that moment.

However this has been becoming pretty much impossible. I get into any game with my friends, and I instantly start tabbing out to do random things.

It might be because I have started associating gaming with just wasting your time pointlessly looking at your computer screen and constantly holding wasd on your keyboard.

I have been finding more enjoyment creating games, more than playing them. But the thing is, whenever I create my own game that I think is really fun, I get bored of it really quickly too.

Well why game then?

Yeah, why? Why am I forcing myself to game when I don't want to? Well I'm not anymore, as of today.

Will you still see me in PopsicL's videos?

If it's related to gaming (which is mainly what his channel is about), then no.

Anyways, thanks to PopsicL for letting me have one of the entries in his journal thing. I also like how this message is going to be in a section "random stuff", when this is completely game changing (pun intended) for his channel.

And if you're interested in what I'm going to do in the future, you can visit my profile on Github at https://github.com/khamper.

ok bye everybody
